About Us

We are college students currently dealing with multiple financial struggles and trying to survive college. Unfortunately, we do not the solution our financial struggles (even though we want to) but we do have a small solution to make money. This is BookDaddy.

Our goal with this project is to have an accessible market dedicated for students to buy and sell books, dorm appliances, and any other product they desire.

We came up with this idea by seeing the need to connect students that were trying to sell their used books to those who were looking for cheap books.
